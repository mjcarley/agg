Evaluate a point on a surface

Evaluate a point on a surface. The surface must have been initialized with agg_surface_weights_make, and its transform initialized with agg_expression_data_compile and agg_transform_expressions_compile, to set up the internal variables and expressions used for point transformation. The section data for S are interpolated at and the point on the section evaluated at . The point is then transformed using the surface transformation.
