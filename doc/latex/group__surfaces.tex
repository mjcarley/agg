Evaluate a point on a surface

Evaluate a point on a surface. The surface must have been initialized with \doxyref{agg\+\_\+surface\+\_\+weights\+\_\+make}{p.}{group__surfaces_ga05f8b5b0febe31ce57337e7045c04903}, and its transform initialized with \doxyref{agg\+\_\+expression\+\_\+data\+\_\+compile}{p.}{group__variables_gac423c9a2a65b0b8e677576e9e4fbb1b6} and \doxyref{agg\+\_\+transform\+\_\+expressions\+\_\+compile}{p.}{group__transforms_ga015c632cb62cafdc579df29f44d8eab9}, to set up the internal variables and expressions used for point transformation. The section data for {\itshape S} are interpolated at $u$ and the point on the section evaluated at $v$. The point is then transformed using the surface transformation.
