Split an edge, the line segment joining two points in $(s,t)$ parametric plane, remapping as required to respect the underlying mapping of the patch. For a linear patch $s=s_{0}+a(s_{1}-s_{0})$, $t=t_{0}+a(t_{1}-t_{0})$; for other mappings, such as spherical or hemispherical, the interpolation is different.
